Why are my taxes different?

The Federal Income Tax system is designed to be a pay as you go system. For traditional wage earners that recieve a W-2, withholding is taken out of wages as earned. Small business owners do not receive wages and thus must pay quarterly taxes to be consistent with the system. Reference

Packages

Remembering all the filing requirements for your federal income taxes can be cumbersome.
We offer several packages based on how often you need reminders. Please peruse our offerings and select the best one for your business.


Annually

We will remind you by December 31st of each year to consolidate all your information for the upcoming filing year.
This is great for small businesses that already have an established accounting system utilized throughout the year.


Semi-Annually

You will receive two reminders within the calendar year: June 30th and December 31st. This is for businesses that need a reminder to gather their relevant tax information such as expenses and receipts at the halfway point and end of the year.
This is also good for businesses that have an established accounting system but may need a quick reminder at the half year point to stay on top of things.

Quarterly

These business will receive reminders that coincide with the required quarterly tax obligations. Reminders will be sent on the following dates: March 31st, June 30th, September 30th and December 31st. These reminder dates represent the end of each quarter and a month before the quarterly filings are due. reference
This is good for businesses that may or may not have an established accounting system but want to make sure they are compliant each quarter and want regular established reminders.

Monthly

These small businesses will receive updates at the end of each month. The reminders will instruct the business owner to aggregate expenses and receipts for the given month. This will be extremely helpful in maintaining accurate and updated records and eliminate the search for old information when it is time to file.
This is great for businesses without a formalized accounting system. Monthly reminders will help business owners stay diligent with gathering information needed for quarterly tax compliance.

Email Reminders

SmallBizzWizz will send reminders for up to two emails of your choosing. When you register for your account you may select to add both a main an alternative email account for reminders. If you have a bookkeeper/accountant/assistant the alternative email is a great way to make sure they receive the reminders as well.

Text Reminders

We know that small business owners are often on the move. Therefore we also offer a text reminder option so that you can receive updates where ever YOU are. Please add the cell phone number where you would like to receive messages when you register for your account. Message and data rates may apply.
